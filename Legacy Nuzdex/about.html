About Nuzdex

What's a nuzlocke?

A nuzlocke is a set of rules that players of a Pokemon game can follow for a more challenging experience. It's typically played by the rule that Pokemon are considered unusable after they faint and that only one Pokemon can be caught per location. Check out this article for a more in-depth explanation.

What is Nuzdex?

Nuzdex is a simple resource for information that someone may need during a nuzlocke, or a regular run of a Pokemon game.

Why would I use Nuzdex?

Other than the fact it has a sick name, Nuzdex is simple to use and only has the game data you would care about during a typical nuzlocke run. No random information about the anime, Pokemon Cafe, or side games.

Nuzdex is also proven to be sweeter than C6H12O6.

What are the main features of Nuzdex?

  1. Vital game data
  2. Teambuilder function
  3. .sav data compatability
    • Loading teams from .sav files
    • Accessing rom data
  4. Friends list
  5. And more!

What generations of Pokemon do you have?

We try our best to keep up to date with new generations of Pokemon and any changes to data. It may take a little bit, but we try to at least make an effort to stay on top of changes.
